1. Introduction

Welcome to EasyFile ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application EasyFile (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices. Please read this privacy policy carefully.

Key Principle: Your privacy is our priority. We do NOT collect, store, transmit, or have access to any of your personal files or documents. All processing happens locally on your device.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Files and Documents - WE COLLECT NOTHING

We do NOT collect, access, store, or transmit your files in any way.

  • PDF Conversion: Performed entirely on your device
  • Image Compression: Processed locally without any upload
  • File Renaming: Handled completely offline on your device
  • No Cloud Storage: We don't use any cloud services
  • No Servers: We don't operate any servers that store your data
  • No Network Transfer: Your files never leave your device

2.2 Personal Information - WE COLLECT NOTHING

We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information, including:

  • No names, emails, or phone numbers
  • No user accounts or login credentials
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, etc.)
  • No contact lists or calendar data
  • No browsing history or search queries

2.3 Usage Analytics - WE COLLECT NOTHING

We do NOT use any analytics services. We do not collect:

  • No crash reports
  • No usage statistics
  • No performance metrics
  • No feature usage tracking

2.4 Device Information - WE COLLECT NOTHING

We do NOT collect any device information. The app only accesses device capabilities necessary for basic functionality (e.g., screen size for UI display), but this information is never collected or transmitted.

3. How We Use Your Information

We don't collect any information, so we don't use any information.

The app's sole purpose is to provide local file processing tools:

  • Convert images to PDF - processed locally on your device
  • Compress images - processed locally on your device
  • Rename files in batch - processed locally on your device

All operations happen entirely within your device's storage. No data is sent anywhere.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 100% Local Processing

Everything happens on your device:

  • All file operations are performed locally
  • Processed files are saved to your device's app-specific directory
  • No internet connection is required or used for file processing
  • We have zero access to your files
  • Your files are never transmitted over any network

4.2 How We Access Your Files

Privacy-Friendly File Access:

  • You choose: You select files through Android's system file picker
  • Temporary access only: The app only accesses files you explicitly choose
  • No broad permissions: We don't request storage permissions that allow access to all your files
  • Compliant with Google Play: Uses Storage Access Framework (SAF) as recommended by Google

4.3 Where Processed Files Are Saved

Processed files are saved to the app's private directory on your device:

  • Location: Android/data/com.hzqqb.easyfile/files/Documents/
  • You can access these files through the app's "Share" feature
  • Files are automatically cleaned up when you uninstall the app
  • No other apps can access this directory without your permission

5. Permissions We Request

ZERO Storage Permissions Required!

As of version 1.4 (September 2025), our app does NOT request any storage permissions:

  • ❌ NO READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE permission
  • ❌ NO WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE permission
  • ❌ NO MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE permission

5.1 How Does the App Work Without Permissions?

We use Android's Storage Access Framework (SAF):

  • File Selection: You choose files through Android's built-in file picker
  • User Control: You are always in control of which files the app can access
  • No Broad Access: The app only accesses files you explicitly select
  • Privacy First: This is the most privacy-friendly way to handle files on Android
  • Google Recommended: This method complies with Google Play's privacy policies

5.2 Why This Is Better for Your Privacy

  • The app can't scan or access all your files
  • You grant access file-by-file, not to your entire storage
  • No permission popup required
  • More transparent and secure
