EasyFile Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024
Effective Date: December 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to EasyFile ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application EasyFile (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Files and Documents

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit your files. All file processing (PDF conversion, image compression, batch renaming) is performed locally on your device. Your files never leave your device and are not uploaded to our servers or any third-party services.

2.2 Usage Analytics (Optional)

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our app, including:

  • App crashes and performance data
  • Feature usage frequency (without identifying specific files)
  • App version and device information
  • General usage patterns

This data is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify you personally.

2.3 Device Information

We may collect basic device information such as:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version
  • Screen resolution and device capabilities

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Process your files locally on your device
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Processing

All file operations are performed locally on your device. We do not have access to your files, and they are not transmitted over the internet.

4.2 Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect any data we collect, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication

5. Permissions We Request

Our app requests the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: To read and save files for processing (PDF conversion, image compression, batch renaming)
  • File Management: To organize and manage your files locally

These permissions are necessary for the app's core functionality and are not used for data collection purposes.

6. Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal information with third parties. The app operates independently and does not integrate with external services that would require data sharing.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Data Retention

We retain anonymous usage data only as long as necessary to provide our services and improve the app. You can clear all app data at any time through your device settings.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal information we may have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of data collection (if applicable)
  • Withdraw consent at any time

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

11. Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Google Play Store requirements
  • Android privacy guidelines

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through:

  • Google Play Store: Visit our app page and use the "Contact Developer" option

We will respond to your inquiries within 48 hours during business days.
